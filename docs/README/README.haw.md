# WhisperColab

Nā puke puke ʻōlelo Google Colabno Whisperkope.

## Nā hiʻohiʻona

- **Hoʻohana manuahi:** Ke hoʻohana nei kēia puke leka [openai/whisper](https://github.com/openai/whisper), i wehe ʻia e OpenAIma Ianuali 17, 2023, me ka Google Colabka GPUmanawa holo.
- **ʻAʻohe palena nui o ka waihona ʻaoʻao puke puke:** Hiki ke hana ʻia nā faila leo nui a me nā wikiō.
- **ʻO ka hana pūʻulu:** Hiki ke hana ʻia nā faila leo a me nā wikiō i ka holo hoʻokahi.
- **Google Drivekākoʻo:** Hiki ke hoʻouka pololei ʻia nā faila, a i ʻole nā faila leo/wikiō i mālama ʻia ma Google Drivehiki ke hana.
- **Paʻa pūnaewele:** Hana ʻia nā hoʻoiho a me ka hana ʻana ma Google Colab. ʻAʻole hoʻohana ʻia nā kumuwaiwai helu kūloko.
- **Nui nā puka puka:** Hoʻopuka ka puke puke i ka palapala mua JSONa loaʻa mai TXT, CSV, Markdown, Excel, a me SRTnā waihona subtitle.

## Hoʻolālā waihona

```text
.github/
  workflows/
    ci.yml
docs/
  README/
    README.<language-code>.md
locales/
  *.json
notebook_template/
  Whisper_Colab.template.ipynb
notebooks/
  Whisper_Colab_<language-code>.ipynb
scripts/
  build_notebooks.py
  build_readmes.py
  check_notebooks.py
  languages.py
  repair_translated_locales.py
  sync_locales.py
  translate_full_locales.py
.gitattributes
.gitignore
LICENSE
README.md
```

ʻO ke kumu READMEka wahi komo ʻōlelo Pelekania, a ʻo docs/READMEnā faila READMEi haku ʻia. ʻO ka puke maʻamau ka puke puke kumu a loaʻa nā mea hoʻokomo e like me `{{cell1.title}}`. Hāʻawi nā faila kūloko i ka ʻōlelo kikoʻī READMEa me Colab i mua i ke kope. Hoʻopaʻa ʻia nā puke i hana ʻia no laila e kūpaʻa nā loulou Colab.

## Kukulu

```bash
python scripts/sync_locales.py
python scripts/build_readmes.py
python scripts/build_notebooks.py
```

## Nānā

```bash
python scripts/check_notebooks.py
```

Hoʻokumu hou ka CI workflow READMEi nā faila a me nā puke mai ka template a me nā faila kūloko, e nānā i ka puke puke JSON, e hōʻoia i nā puka ʻole, e nānā i ka Python syntax i loko o nā cell code, e ʻimi i nā mea huna i ʻike ʻole ʻia, a hāʻule i ka wā i hana ʻole ʻia nā faila.

## He aha ka CI hiki ke ho'āʻo

- READMEhanauna mai nā faila kūloko
- hana puke puke mai ka la'ana a me ka waihona wahi
- puke puke JSONono
- nā puka hoʻokō hakahaka
- ʻO ka syntax Python i nā pūnaewele code
- mea huna pohihihi maopopo
- hana ʻia READMEa me nā waihona puke e pili ana i nā kumu

Pono ke ala unuhi piha i nā API kikoʻī Colab, nā hoʻoiho hoʻohālike, GPUloaʻa, a me nā faila media i hāʻawi ʻia e ka mea hoʻohana. E mālama i kēlā ala e like me ka hoʻāʻo ʻana i ka uahi Colab manual ma hope o ka hala ʻana o nā loiloi i hana ʻia.
