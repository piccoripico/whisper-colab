# WhisperColab

Multilingual Google Colablitattafan rubutu don Whisperrubutun.

## Siffofin

- **Kyauta don amfani:** Wannan littafin rubutu yana amfani da [openai/whisper](https://github.com/openai/whisper), wanda OpenAIbude-sourced ranar 17 ga Janairu, 2023, tare da Google Colab's free GPUruntime.
- **Babu ƙayyadadden iyaka girman fayil ɗin gefen littafin rubutu:** Ana iya sarrafa manyan fayilolin odiyo da bidiyo.
- **sarrafa tsari:** Ana iya sarrafa fayilolin odiyo da bidiyo da yawa a cikin gudu ɗaya.
- **Google Drivetallafi:** Ana iya loda fayiloli kai tsaye, ko fayilolin odiyo/bidiyo da aka adana a Google Driveana iya sarrafa su.
- **Cikakken kan layi:** Ana yin zazzagewa da sarrafawa akan Google Colab. Ba a yi amfani da albarkatun kwamfuta na gida ba.
- **Multiple fitarwa Formats:** Littafin bayanin kula yana fitar da asalin JSONkwafin kuma an samu TXT, CSV, Markdown, Excel, da SRTfayilolin subtitle.

## Tsarin Ma'ajiya

```text
.github/
  workflows/
    ci.yml
docs/
  README/
    README.<language-code>.md
locales/
  *.json
notebook_template/
  Whisper_Colab.template.ipynb
notebooks/
  Whisper_Colab_<language-code>.ipynb
scripts/
  build_notebooks.py
  build_readmes.py
  check_notebooks.py
  languages.py
  repair_translated_locales.py
  sync_locales.py
  translate_full_locales.py
.gitattributes
.gitignore
LICENSE
README.md
```

Tushen READMEshine wurin shigarwa na Ingilishi, kuma docs/READMEya ƙunshi fayilolin READMEda aka samar. Littafin rubutu na samfuri shine tushen littafin rubutu kuma ya ƙunshi masu riƙe wuri kamar `{{cell1.title}}`. Fayilolin gida suna ba da takamaiman yare READMEda kwafi mai fuskantar Colab. An ƙaddamar da littattafan rubutu da aka ƙirƙira don haka hanyoyin haɗin gwiwar Colab su tabbata.

## Gina

```bash
python scripts/sync_locales.py
python scripts/build_readmes.py
python scripts/build_notebooks.py
```

## Dubawa

```bash
python scripts/check_notebooks.py
```

Gudun aikin CI yana sake ginawa READMEfayiloli da litattafan rubutu daga samfuri da fayilolin gida, duba littafin rubutu JSON, yana tabbatar da abubuwan da ba komai ba, bincika syntax na Python a cikin sel ɗin lamba, bincika sirrin sirri na haɗari, kuma yana kasawa lokacin da fayilolin da aka ƙirƙira ba su aikata ba.

## Abin da CI zai iya Gwaji

- READMEtsara daga fayilolin gida
- tsara littafin rubutu daga samfuri da fayilolin gida
- littafin rubutu JSONinganci
- komai kisa
- Python syntax a cikin sel code
- bayyanannen sirrin bazata
- ƙaddamar da READMEda fayilolin littafin rubutu da suka dace da tushen

Cikakken hanyar rubutun yana buƙatar takamaiman APIs na Colab, zazzagewar ƙira, samuwan GPU, da fayilolin mai jarida da aka samar. Bi da waccan hanyar azaman gwajin hayaƙin Colab na hannu bayan ƙetare-fayil cak.
